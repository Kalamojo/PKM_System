# Covariance

> [!note]
> A measurement of the association between two attributes $X_1$ and $X_2$.

> [!math]
> $$\begin{aligned}
\sigma_{12} &=E\left[\left(X_{1}-\mu_{1}\right)\left(X_{2}-\mu_{2}\right)\right] \\
&=E\left[X_{1} X_{2}\right]-E\left[X_{1}\right] E\left[X_{2}\right]
\end{aligned}$$
> $E[(X - \mu)] =$ variance
