# Union


> [!note]
> The union of two sets $A$ and $B$, denoted $A \cup B$, is all sets that are a part of $A$ or $B$ or both.

> [!example] 
> $\{1,2,3\} \cup \{2,3,4\} = \{1,2,3,4\}$
