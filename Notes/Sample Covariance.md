# Sample Covariance

```ad-note
A measure of the association between two samples of attributes.
```

```ad-math
$$\hat{\sigma}_{1 2} = \frac{1}{n-1} \sum^n_{i=1}(x_{i 1} - \hat{\mu}_{1})(x_{i 2} - \hat{\mu}_{2})$$
```
