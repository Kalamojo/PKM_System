# Superset
> [!note]
> $B$ is a superset of $A$ provided $A$ is a subset of $B$. Denoted $B \supset A$


> [!example] 
> $\{1,2,3,4,5\} \text{ is a superset of } \{2,4\}$


$3 \superset 3$
