# Existential Quantifier

> [!note]
> Represented as "there exists" or $\exists$.

> [!example] 
> - $\exists x Q(x,5) = \text{there exists an x that is larger than 5}$
> 	- 10
> - $\exists c P(c) = \text{there exists a country that has a president}$
> 	- United States
