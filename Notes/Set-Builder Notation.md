# Set-Builder Notation

> [!example] 
> - $\{\frac{m}{n} | m,n\epsilon \mathbb{Z}, n \neq 0\}:$
>	 -  $\{\frac{-2}{1},\frac{5}{7},\frac{3}{-2},...\}$
> - $2\mathbb{Z}=$
>	 -  $\{...,-4,-2,0,2,4,...\}$
>	 - $\{2n | n\epsilon \mathbb{Z}\}$
> - $\text{Desk} =$
>	- $\{\text{drink, laptop, microphone}\}$
>	- $\{x | x \text{ is on my desk}\}$
