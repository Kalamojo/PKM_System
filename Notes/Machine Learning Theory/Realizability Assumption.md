# Realizability Assumption

>[!note]
> The assumption that [[Existential Quantifier|there exists]] a perfect [[Function|Hypothesis]] within a [[Hypothesis Class]], such that true [[Error Term|Error]] is 0.

> [!math]
> $$\exists h^\star \in \mathcal{H}$$
> s.t.
> $$\text{err}_{\mathcal{D},f}(h^\star)=0$$
