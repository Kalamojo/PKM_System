# VC Dimension

>[!note]
> The maximum [[Cardinality]] of [[Training Set]] $S$ that is still [[Shattered Set|Shattered]] by [[Hypothesis Class]] $\mathcal{H}$.

> [!math]
> $$\text{VCDim}(\mathcal{H})=\text{max}\{|S|: S \text{ shattered by }\mathcal{H}\}$$
