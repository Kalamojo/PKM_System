# Sample Variance

> [!note]
> The expectation of the squared distance of data points from the mean of the data set.

> [!math]
> $S^2 = \frac{\sum\left(x_{i}-\bar{x}\right)^{2}}{n-1}$
> $x_i = \text{value of sample item}$
> $\bar{x} = \text{sample mean}$
> $n = \text{sample size}$
