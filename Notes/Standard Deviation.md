# Standard Deviation

> [!note]
> A measure of the dispertion of a set of values. Also calculated as the positive square root of the variance.

> [!math]
> $\sigma=\sqrt{E[(X - \mu)^2]}$
> $N = \text{population size}$
> $x_i = \text{value from the population}$
> $\mu = \text{population mean}$
