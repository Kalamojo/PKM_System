# Undirected Graph
> [!note]
> A graph where edges ($E$) connect certain vertices ($V$) without direction.

> [!math]
> $G = (V,E)$
> $E \subseteq \{\{a,b\}: a,b \in V\}$

> [!example] 
> $G = (\{a,b,c,d\}, \{\{a,b\},\{b,c\},\{c,d\},\{b,d\}\})$
