# Cartesian Product


> [!note]
> The set of all ordered pairs ($a$, $b$) from sets $A$ and $B$, denoted $A \cdot B$, 

> [!example] 
> $\{2,3\} \cdot \{x,y\} = \{(2,x), (2,y), (3,x), (3,y)\}$
