# Factorial

> [!note]
> Is a way to evaluate the number of ways to rearrange a number of objects.
> Multiplies a discrete number by the same number - 1 until the number equals 1.

> [!math]
> $n! = n*(n-1)*(n-2)*...*3*2*1$
