## Data Storytelling

---
> Make it easy for the audience to get the point, like in 10-15 seconds. Often makes use of [[Data Visualization]]

---
## Qualities

- Clutter free
- Highly organized

---
## Audience

- Nonanalysts
- Nontechnical business managers
